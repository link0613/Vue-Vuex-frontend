  1. Log into your Linkedin account.
  2. Move your cursor over Profile in the top menu and click "Edit Profile".
  3. Click "Edit Contact Info" near the bottom right of the profile overview section.
  4. Click the pencil icon next to Websites. Choose a type from the dropdown list.
  5. Copy and paste your Jobdone url into the URL field and click "Save".
  1. Log into your Twitter account.
  2. Click on your Profile in the top menu.
  3. Mouse hover your avatar and click "Edit".
  4. Copy and paste your Jobdone url into the Website field and click "Save changes".
  1. Log into your Facebook account.
  2. Click your name in the upper left corner of the homepage to access your profile.
  3. Click the pencil icon of the "About" section and then Update Info.
  4. Click "Edit" in the "Contact Information" section.
  5. Copy and paste your Jobdone url into the Website field and click "Save".
  1. Log into your Google Plus account.
  2. Click Profile on the left.
  3. On the "About" tab, click Edit under Links.
  4. Select "Add custom link" under "Other Profiles".
  5. Copy and paste your Jobdone url into the URL field and add a label for your profile for example "Jobdone Profile".
  6. Click Save.
  1. Log into your Behance account.
  2. Mouse hover your name in the top menu and click "My Profile".
  3. Go to "Edit Profile".
  4. Copy and paste your Jobdone url into the Website URL field under "Personal Information".
  5. Click Save.
